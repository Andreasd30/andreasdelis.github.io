Intro

As an accomplished graduate of AUEB with a BSc in Economics specializing in Finance and Accounting, I stand out with a distinction in BSc in Accounting and Finance.

My journey includes a year in the prestigious Big-4 audit department, shaping my expertise and fostering multi-faceted soft skills essential for success in the financial realm.

I am now seeking an opportunity in the financial sector to leverage my profound knowledge and honed abilities, contributing effectively to a dynamic environment and fueling my passion for continued growth and innovation.

Work

I have accumulated diverse and robust experience in accounting and financial analysis through various roles in Greece.

Currently, I serve as a Staff Accountant at KPMG Certificated Auditors S.A., where I am responsible for executing fundamental audit procedures such as treasury and debt management, revenue and expense tracking, and inventory auditing. My work involves direct client interaction, with significant projects in the industry of Energy and Automotive.

Prior to this, I worked as a Financial Analyst/Accountant at the Army Financial Service Department (401 GSNA), where I managed multi-million-dollar invoices using SAP software and conducted balance agreements with suppliers to aid in financial policy formulation.

My earlier experience includes a role as an Accountant in a private accounting office, where I managed invoice entries, receipts, and tax returns for various companies.

Additionally, I served as an Evaluator at the Hellenic Gaming Commission, assessing company applications for licensing in the betting sector, analyzing share compositions and balance sheets to determine financial viability.

These positions have equipped me with a comprehensive skill set in auditing, financial management, and regulatory compliance.

Education

My educational background is grounded in economics, with degrees from two prestigious universities.

I earned a Bachelor of Science in Economics from the Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB), graduating in the top 10% of my class with a GPA of 7.72/10. My studies at AUEB included core courses such as Statistics, Microeconomic and Macroeconomic Theory, Financial Accounting, Econometrics, Investment Analysis, and Applied Industrial Analysis.

I furthered my education at the University of Cyprus (UCY), where I also earned a Bachelor of Science in Economics, achieving a GPA of 9/10 and ranking in the top 3% of my class. My coursework at UCY covered Econometric Methods, Advanced Business English, International Trade, International Finance, and Labor Economics. Additionally.

I have also pursued professional certification with ICAEW, completing the Certificate Level with courses in Assurance, Accounting, and Business Technology & Finance. This comprehensive educational foundation has equipped me with a strong analytical and quantitative skill set, essential for my professional development in the fields of economics and finance.
